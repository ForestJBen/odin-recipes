Chicken Pie recipe:

Description:

A classic dish that is loved by all the family and is easy and fun to make

Ingredients:

  • 2 x 320g rolled pastry
  • 3 chicken breasts
  • 3 carrots
  • 3 medium sized potatoes
  • Pinch of salt and pepper
  • 1 tsp of drie thyme
  • 600 ml chicken stock
  • 50g butter
  • 1 large onion
  • 6 tblespoon flour
  • 300ml milk
  • juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 egg

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 200C
  2. Line a 10 x 8 inch pie dish with one roll of pastry
  3. Prick base several times with a fork
  4. Liine pastry with baking parchment
  5. Place in oven for ten minutes
  6. Place chicken, carrots, potatoes, salt , pepper,thyme and stock into large saucepan
  7. Bring to boil then allow to simmer for 15 minutes
  8. Place onion and butter in another saucepan and heat for 5 -6 minutes
  9. Add flour to onion mix and mix using balloon whisk
  10. Stir broth mixture into onion mixture
  11. Pour in milk and stir with whisk while heating
  12. Add in chicken and veggies
  13. Bring back to boil and add lemon juice
  14. Remove baking beans and baking parchment and fill with chicken mixture
  15. Brush edges with egg wash and add remaining pastry on top
  16. Pinch edges to seal, brush top with egg wash and poke holes in top with knife
  17. Place in oven for 20-25 minutes
  18. Serve with veggies
