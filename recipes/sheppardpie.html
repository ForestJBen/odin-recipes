Shepherds Pie recipe

Description:

Classic family dish consisting of potatoes, meat and thick gravy perfect to lift anyones mood.

Ingredients:

  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 large peeled and quartered potatoes
  • 8 tablespoons butter
  • 1 medium onion chopped
  • 2 cups mixed vegatables
  • 1 1/2 ground beef
  • 1/2 cup beef broth
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire Sauce
  • Additional spices and needed

Steps:

  1. Boil potatoes
  2. Preheat oven to 400^0F
  3. Saute vegetables
  4. Add ground beef and sauces
  5. Mash potatoes
  6. Layer meat mixture and potatoes
  7. Bake
